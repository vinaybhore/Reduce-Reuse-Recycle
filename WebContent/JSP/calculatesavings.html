How many trees are necessary to make a given amount of paper ?


Facts displayed on this page is based on published reports from sources such as printer manufacturers, government statistics and paper manufacturers.

Paper Facts

One tree makes 8333 sheets of copy paper


One ton of coated non-recycled lower-end Newsmagzine and catalog paper uses 8 trees


One ton of coated, non-recycled higher-end magzine paper(used for magazines like National Geographic) uses 15 trees

One ton of non-recycled newsprint uses 12 trees

1 ton of uncoated virgin (non-recycled) printing and office paper uses 24 trees
